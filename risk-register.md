# Risk Register

| Risk | Impact | Probability | Mitigation |
|------|--------|------------|------------|
| Users resist new CRM | High | Medium | Provide training and guides |
| Data migration errors | High | Low | Test data before go-live |
| System downtime | Medium | Low | Schedule off-hour deployment |
| Delayed requirements | Medium | Medium | Weekly stakeholder reviews |
| Low user adoption | High | Medium | Management support and KPI tracking |
