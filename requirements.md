## Business Problem
Sales teams are unable to track customer leads properly due to scattered Excel sheets and manual processes.

## Goals
- Centralize all customer data
- Improve sales follow-up
- Increase lead conversion
- Enable real-time reporting

## Functional Requirements
1. Users must be able to create and edit leads
2. Leads must be assigned automatically to sales reps
3. Sales reps must be able to update lead status
4. Managers must see daily sales dashboards
5. Customer contact history must be stored

## Non-Functional Requirements
- System should be cloud-based
- Data should be secure
- System should be accessible remotely
